Welcome to Scogs' Rocks and Roll

A brand new Greenpoint gym, community space, bar, and live music venue.

During the Day...Bouldering

  • The gym features 7,000 sq. ft. of climbing terrain, including walls sloping at 15, 30, 45, and 60 degrees.
  • Weight Room with two Pelotons and three squat racks.
  • Limited to ages 13 and up.

But at Night...Heavy Metal

  • An orgy of terror, guitar riffs, and 70 beers on tap.
  • Voted best new metal bar (NY Mag, February 2020).
  • Friday-Saturday nights: Scogs' Sausage bar featuring rotating saausages from local NY farms.
